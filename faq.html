Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about Murphy Wellness

Use this page for quick answers about membership, insurance, self-pay visits, appointments, communication, and clinic logistics.

Membership

Membership questions

Membership is designed for patients who want ongoing, relationship-based primary care with more continuity and easier access over time.

Membership is $65/month for adults 18+ and $25/month for children with an adult parent or guardian who is also a member.

No. Murphy Wellness also offers insurance-based visits and one-time self-pay visits.

Insurance

Insurance questions

Yes. Murphy Wellness is in network with Cigna, United/UMR, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Ambetter/Wellcare, Medicaid, and Medicare.

Yes. Coverage and patient responsibility vary by plan, so the clinic can help explain your options as clearly as possible.

Self-pay

Self-pay questions

Yes. One-time self-pay visits are available for patients who do not want a membership.

Visits are $65 for under 20 minutes, $130 for 20-40 minutes, and $195 for 41+ minutes.

Yes. If you convert the following month, your self-pay visit amount can be credited toward each month paid.

Appointments

Appointment questions

You can call, text, or use the contact form to request a visit.

Yes. Tuesday hours are shifted to 3-9 PM for patients who need access after work.

Communication and logistics

Contact and location questions

Yes. Telehealth visits are available for all patients at the same rates as in-person visits.
