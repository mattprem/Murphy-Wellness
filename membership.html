Membership

Membership-based primary care for patients who want a closer care relationship.

Murphy Wellness membership is designed for patients who want ongoing primary care with more continuity, more direct access, and fewer surprises. It is a simple way to build your care around relationship, time, and consistency.

How it works

Membership is designed to be straightforward.

1. Join the membership

Choose membership if you want ongoing, relationship-based primary care rather than a one-time visit model.

2. Schedule and stay connected

Use the clinic for routine care, follow-up, telehealth, and ongoing primary care needs.

3. Build continuity over time

Membership works best for patients who value continuity, communication, and a provider who knows their health history.

Member-only benefits

Cash-price access to labs, medications, and imaging

One of the more practical advantages of membership is access to cash-price discounts on services ordered through the clinic. These rates are not available to non-members.

Lab work

Lab orders placed through the clinic are billed at cash-price rates for members — typically much lower than what a standard office bills through insurance.

Medications

Members can access medications at cash-price rates, including weight loss medications. Non-members are billed directly by the pharmacy at that pharmacy's rates — through insurance or otherwise.

Radiology and imaging

Imaging services ordered through Murphy Wellness are available to members at cash-price rates rather than being routed through standard billing.

For non-members: Outside services — labs, medications, imaging, and other third-party services — are billed directly to the patient by the outside company (lab, pharmacy, imaging center, etc.) at the rates those companies set, whether through insurance or otherwise. Membership is required to access cash-price discounts on these services.
What membership is designed to provide

What’s included

  • Ongoing primary care support
  • Longer visits when needed
  • A stronger long-term care relationship
  • More direct communication and follow-up
  • Telehealth at the same rates as in-person care
  • Cash-price access to labs, imaging, and medications — including weight loss medications
  • Clear expectations around care and access
Why patients choose it

Membership may be a good fit if you:

  • Want a primary care home, not just a one-time visit
  • Value having more time with your clinician
  • Want easier access and continuity over time
  • Prefer a more personal care experience
  • Live locally and want a more connected clinic relationship

One-time visits can transition

Start with a self-pay visit if you need to.

If you begin with a one-time self-pay visit and decide in the following month that you want membership, Murphy Wellness can credit those paid visit amounts toward your membership months. That gives patients a low-pressure way to start care and decide what fits after the visit.
