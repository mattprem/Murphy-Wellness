What makes care different here

Relationship-based care changes the experience.

Many patients are used to short visits, long waits, and feeling like they have to squeeze everything into a rushed appointment. Murphy Wellness is built differently. The focus is on relationship-based care, thoughtful follow-up, and practical access so patients can feel heard, understood, and better supported over time.

Longer visits and better access

More time. Better conversations. Stronger continuity.

  • Longer visits create room for better questions and clearer plans.
  • Telehealth is available for all patients at the same rates.
  • Tuesday evening hours run from 3-9 PM for patients who need access after work.
  • The clinic can help you figure out whether membership, insurance, or self-pay is the best fit.
Rural and community-centered care

Care shaped by real community needs.

Murphy Wellness is rooted in the realities of rural care. Access matters. Convenience matters. Being known by your clinic matters. This practice is designed to serve patients who want high-quality primary care close to home, with a more personal and community-centered feel.

Flexible care options may be available depending on the situation and what is clinically appropriate. Contact the clinic to ask what visit formats may be best for your needs.
