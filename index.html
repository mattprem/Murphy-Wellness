Three ways to get care

Choose the care path that fits you.

You should be able to understand your options quickly. These three pathways are the core of the new patient experience.

Best for ongoing care

Membership

Designed for patients who want a long-term primary care relationship, easier access, and simple monthly pricing.

  • $65/month for adults 18+
  • $25/month for children with a participating parent or guardian
  • Helpful for patients who value continuity and direct communication

Best if you want to use insurance

Insurance-Based Visits

Use traditional billing with Murphy Wellness if you prefer standard coverage-based visits.

  • In network with Cigna, United/UMR, BCBS, Aetna, Ambetter/Wellcare, Medicaid, and Medicare
  • Telehealth visits available at the same rates
  • Great fit for patients who want to use existing benefits

Membership snapshot

A simpler way to stay connected to your care.

For many patients, membership is the easiest path to ongoing care. It is especially helpful if you want continuity, easier access, and a stronger long-term relationship with your clinic.

  • $65/month for adults 18+
  • $25/month for children with a member parent or guardian

Insurance and self-pay snapshot

Prefer insurance or need one-time pricing?

Murphy Wellness also offers in-network insurance visits and one-time self-pay visits. If you do not have insurance and do not want a membership, you can still get care with clear visit-based pricing.

  • In network with major plans including BCBS, Aetna, Cigna, United/UMR, Medicaid, and Medicare
  • Telehealth visits available at the same rates
  • Self-pay visits can be credited toward membership if you join the following month
