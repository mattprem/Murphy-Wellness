For small businesses

Health coverage for your team at membership rates

Murphy Wellness can partner with local small businesses to provide primary care for employees and their families. Through an employer arrangement, your team accesses the same membership rates — making real health coverage practical without the overhead of traditional group insurance.

  • $65/month per adult employee or family member
  • $25/month per dependent child
  • Longer visits, real continuity, and a provider who knows your team
  • Cash-price labs, imaging, and medications included
  • On-site workplace visits available on a limited basis — a meaningful perk for your team
  • No complex insurance administration required
