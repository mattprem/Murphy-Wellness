Insurance and self-pay pricing

Clear pricing options for how you want to receive care.

Murphy Wellness offers three ways to receive care: membership, traditional insurance billing, and one-time self-pay visits. If you do not have insurance and do not want a membership, one-time visits are available with straightforward visit-length pricing.

Contact Us About Pricing Request a Visit

20-40 minutes $130 self-pay visit
41+ minutes $195 self-pay visit
Insurance billing

Want to use insurance?

Murphy Wellness offers traditional insurance billing for patients who want to use their coverage for primary care visits. Coverage and patient responsibility can vary by plan, so the clinic can help you understand what applies to your visit.

  • In network with Cigna
  • In network with United and UMR
  • In network with Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • In network with Aetna
  • In network with Ambetter and Wellcare
  • In network with Medicaid and Medicare
Self-pay

Prefer self-pay instead?

If you do not have insurance, or you do not want a membership, you can still schedule a one-time self-pay visit. Telehealth visits are available for all patients at the same rates as in-person visits.

If you decide in the following month that you want to join membership, your self-pay visit amount can be credited toward each month paid.

Which option may fit you best?

Quick comparison by patient need

Membership

Best for patients who want ongoing care, a stronger long-term relationship, and easier access over time.

Insurance-Based Visits

Best for patients who want to use traditional insurance billing for primary care visits.

One-Time Self-Pay Visits

Best for patients without insurance, or patients who want a one-time visit without joining a membership.
