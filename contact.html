Helpful details before your first visit

  • Hours are 10 AM–6 PM Monday and Wednesday–Friday, with Tuesday evening appointments from 3–9 PM. Saturday hours may be offered by demand — contact us to ask.
  • Walk-ins are welcome. If Jeni is out on a visit, give the clinic a call and she will let you know when she is back. Calling ahead is always ideal.
  • Telehealth visits are available for all patients at the same rates.
  • If you are unsure whether membership, insurance, or one-time self-pay is best, contact us first.
