Aesthetic services

Microneedling at Murphy Wellness

A straightforward treatment that uses controlled micro-injuries to prompt your skin to rebuild collagen and elastin — the proteins that keep it firm and smooth.

Over time, those tiny channels close and the repair process does the work. The result is gradual, real improvement rather than an overnight change.

Free consultations are available — no commitment required. If you have questions about whether microneedling makes sense for your skin, we are happy to talk it through.

What it addresses

Common reasons patients try microneedling

  • Fine lines and early wrinkles
  • Acne scarring and post-acne texture
  • Uneven skin tone or rough texture
  • Enlarged pores
  • Mild pigmentation changes

Results vary by skin type and condition. A consultation is the best way to get a realistic sense of what to expect for your situation.

About the treatment

What actually happens during a session

A device with fine needles creates small, controlled channels in the surface of the skin. Those micro-injuries trigger a natural healing response — your body increases collagen and elastin production in the treated area.

Sessions typically take 30 to 60 minutes. Numbing cream is applied beforehand to keep things comfortable. Most people describe the sensation as mild pressure or warmth.

What to expect

Recovery and results, honestly described

  1. After your session
    Expect redness and mild sensitivity for 24 to 48 hours — similar to a mild sunburn. Most people can return to normal activity the next day. Avoid heavy makeup and direct sun exposure during that window.
  2. Over the following weeks
    Skin texture and tone typically begin to improve as collagen production increases. Changes are gradual — you are unlikely to notice a dramatic difference after one session.
  3. Full results
    The full benefit of a treatment series can take up to six to eight months to appear after your final session. Collagen remodeling continues long after the visible redness resolves.
Recommended series

Four treatments, four weeks apart

For most skin concerns, a series of four sessions spaced about four weeks apart gives the best outcome. Each session builds on the last, and the spacing allows your skin time to recover and respond fully before the next treatment.

That said, some people see meaningful improvement with fewer sessions. The free consultation is a good time to talk through your goals and set realistic expectations.
