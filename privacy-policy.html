Privacy policy

Privacy Policy and Notice of Privacy Practices

Effective date: March 11, 2026. This page explains how Murphy Wellness handles information collected through this website and summarizes how medical information may be used and disclosed under HIPAA.

Website privacy policy

What this website collects

Murphy Wellness may collect information you choose to provide through this website, including your name, phone number, email address, preferred contact method, and the contents of your message. If you contact us by phone, text, email, or website form, we may also keep records of those communications to respond to you and support clinic operations.

How we use website information

We use website information to respond to questions, help schedule appointments, explain membership, insurance, and self-pay options, provide requested forms or portal access details, improve website performance, and maintain the security of our systems.

What not to send through this website

Please do not use this website, standard email, or text messaging for emergencies or for sending detailed, highly sensitive medical information unless Murphy Wellness has specifically instructed you to do so. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911.

Website tools, analytics, and cookies

This website may use basic analytics, server logs, spam-prevention tools, and standard website technologies that help the site operate, measure traffic, and improve usability. These tools may collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, device type, and pages visited. Murphy Wellness does not use website analytics to replace or bypass HIPAA obligations.

Patient portal and third-party links

The patient portal and certain website tools may be provided by third parties. When you leave this website and enter a patient portal, payment system, map provider, or another third-party website, that provider’s privacy and security practices apply to the information you submit there.

How we protect website information

Murphy Wellness uses reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect information collected through this website. No internet transmission or email system is guaranteed to be completely secure, so please use caution when deciding what information to send online.

Children’s privacy

This website is not intended for children to use independently. If information about a child is submitted through the site, Murphy Wellness will treat that information in accordance with applicable law and clinic privacy practices.

Changes to this policy

Murphy Wellness may update this page from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Notice of Privacy Practices

This notice describes how medical information about you may be used and disclosed and how you can get access to this information. Please review it carefully.

Our responsibilities

  • Murphy Wellness is required by law to maintain the privacy and security of your protected health information.
  • We will let you know promptly if a breach occurs that may have compromised the privacy or security of your information.
  • We must follow the duties and privacy practices described in this notice and provide you with a copy upon request.
  • We will not use or share your information other than as described here unless you tell us we can in writing. You may change your mind at any time in writing.

How we may use and disclose your health information

Murphy Wellness may use and disclose your health information for treatment, payment, and health care operations. Examples include coordinating your care, submitting claims to your health plan, obtaining payment, improving quality, training staff, managing the practice, conducting business operations, and maintaining records required by law.

We may also use or disclose information when required or permitted by law, including for public health activities, reporting abuse or neglect, health oversight, judicial or administrative proceedings, law enforcement requests, workers’ compensation, certain government functions, organ and tissue donation, medical examiners or funeral directors, and to avert a serious threat to health or safety when allowed by law.

Your choices about certain disclosures

For certain situations, you may tell Murphy Wellness your preferences about whether we share information with family, close friends, or others involved in your care or payment for your care. If you are unable to tell us your preference and we believe sharing is in your best interest, we may use our professional judgment as allowed by law.

Uses and disclosures that generally require your written authorization

In most cases, Murphy Wellness will ask for your written authorization before using or disclosing psychotherapy notes, using your information for marketing in a way that requires authorization, or selling your health information. If you give authorization, you may revoke it in writing at any time for future use.

Your rights

  • You may ask to inspect or get a copy of your medical record and certain billing information.
  • You may ask us to correct information you believe is incorrect or incomplete.
  • You may request confidential communications, such as asking us to contact you in a specific way or at a specific location.
  • You may ask us to limit certain uses or disclosures. We are not always required to agree, except in limited situations such as certain disclosures to a health plan when you have paid in full out of pocket.
  • You may ask for a list of certain disclosures we have made of your information.
  • You may request a paper copy of this notice at any time, even if you agreed to receive it electronically.
  • If someone is acting for you through legal authority, that person may exercise your rights where permitted by law.

Substance use disorder records

To the extent Murphy Wellness maintains substance use disorder patient records that are subject to 42 CFR Part 2, those records will not be used or disclosed for investigations or legal proceedings against you except as permitted by law, such as with your written consent or a valid court order and subpoena where required.

Questions or complaints

If you have questions about this notice or believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may contact Murphy Wellness at (620) 992-6088 or info@premonition.health. You may also file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights. Murphy Wellness will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint.
